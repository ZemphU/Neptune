The new social movement

As the youth, we have been disproportionately affected by the attention-based business models of the largest tech companies. We've had enough.

Power in numbers

Really, we work for social media companies by giving them our time, attention, and information — but all we get in return? Mental illness and instability on a societal scale. Just like the powerful labor movements of the 20th century, we aim to influence social media through organized boycotts, public pressure, and signal-boosting safer alternatives.

Real choices for real people

We are claiming back our agency by working together. Is there a specific website or app that is taking away your time? Here is a community of people with the exact same goals.

Blue skies

As much as it has so far been used against us, technology can be used for good. By bringing these issues into the public eye on campus and beyond, we're working towards a future where technlogy works for students' — and humanity's — best interests.
